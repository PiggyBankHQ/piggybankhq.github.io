Natural Optical Physics

Key indicators of genuine photography:

  • Identical Catchlights: Twin light reflections match light sources naturally in both pupils.
  • Background Optics: Background blur exhibits consistent depth of field without distortion.
  • Micro-Details: Asymmetrical skin pores and natural, non-uniform hair strand distribution.

Generative Model Anomaly Breakdown

Frequent flaws found in AI renders:

  • Mismatched Pupils & Reflection: Pupil shapes distort or carry non-matching catchlight reflections.
  • Melting Accessories: Jewelry, eyeglasses frames, or collars blend directly into skin or hair.
  • Over-Smoothed Texture: Synthetic plastic skin effect missing microscopic pores or fine wrinkles.
